Who Am I?

Goal

Swarmbots is a system of roughly 2 by 2 by 2 inch cubed robots that can collectively perform a given task by sharing the workload among identical members of the swarmbot system.The bots will first be trained in a virtual environment using ROS. This phase will allow members to explore different neural network architecture as well as different Reinforcement Learning algorithms. The final goal of the project is to have a team of four robots controlled by users play soccer against a team of four reinforcement learning trained robots.

Timeline

  • December 2017- January 2018

    Members begin the project by learning reinforcement learning.

  • February 2018

    Hardware Team assembles the robots and develop the user interface.

    Software Team trains robots in ROS.

  • March 2018

    The robots will be finished.

  • Picnic Day April 21, 2018

    The Swarmbots will be presented in Kemper Hall in UC Davis!

    Come bring your family and friends to play soccer with robots!

  • Post Picnic Day

    The Swarmbots will be taught to spell letters.
